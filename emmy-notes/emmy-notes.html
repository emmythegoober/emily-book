Emmy Note #1 - The World

I have noticed that I’ve been creating stuff without a proper understanding of which countries should exist in Terraferma. I don’t have a map, nor do I have map-making skills, but this doesn’t mean I can create countries willy-nilly. As I’m writing this, I have already created several entries, most of them only mention one nation, Montagna, which is where Emilia, Eliza, the Professoressa, Emilia’s mom, and basically 80% of the people that appear in Emilia’s logs come from.

However, I have a problem when it comes to background nations, the non-Montagna places. I believe I should make a list of these nations, maybe even their continents. It should be something that I can understand, and I don’t mind if other people don’t get this, these are my notes after all. Ok, so, Montagna (which just means “mountain” in Italian, ain’t I clever?) is located on the Padovan continent. Now, Padova is probably gonna be an Europe analogue, but not fully, you know? I can’t just make the Hundred Years’ War but with magic, I’m not JK Rowling, I don’t do lazy worldbuilding, at least I think I don’t.

Then there’s the Mancomunidad, which I am not sure if I want to make it a country. Then there’s Bergia, which should be a sort of neighbour to Montagna, kinda of a US paralel, maybe a Germany paralel, probably Germany. There’s the Kingdom of Nabouro, which João Paulo Reis comes from, I think we can put that in an Africa analogue, maybe Northern Africa, Egypt. The Kishian continent could be the Asia analogue, but I think making it predominantly Middle Eastern or Central Asian could be good.

There’s several ancient nations mentioned in the first entry, like Kashi, Maltos, and Harari, but maybe those nations collapsed and shouldn’t be in the list.
