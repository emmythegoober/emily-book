There is no way to define a "true" history of Terraferma. Every historian has their own biases, their own stories that will infiltrate even the purest of attempts to record history. Don't trust the false historian who says they have the true, unfiltered history; trust the one who admits their faults.

—Welmutt Schmidte, On History

—Manuel de la Mancomunidad, The Ages Forgotten, Act VI, Scene II
