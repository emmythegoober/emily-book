PROLOGUE


If one is to set sail from Montagna and traverse a five hundred leagues of ocean, one will find an archipelago by the name of "la Mancomunidad." To the outsider, the Mancomunidad appears as just another group of islands floating on the Limbiar Ocean, not too different from the Bergian islands, but one would be very mistaken in believing the two would be any similar. Whereas Bergia sits upon a natural archipelago, formed from volcanic rock and millennia of geological activity, the Mancomunidad sits atop a former mountain, its peak has been shaved off and rifts have opened throughout forming the nation's islands. Whereas Bergia has moved past the Crisis Year, the Mancomunidad cannot. For them, the Crisis Year will never pass, they won't allow it to.
